# Hospital Emergency Room - Data Analysis (Interactive Dashboard creation using MS Excel)
## Project Objective
To analyze hospital emergency room data and create an interactive Excel dashboard that tracks key KPIs like patient flow, wait times, and satisfaction score.
It helps hospital management make data-driven decisions and improve overall efficiency.

## Dataset used
- <a href="https://github.com/vipin-s27/Hospital-ER-Data-Analysis/blob/main/Hospital_ER_Dashboard.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)
-	How many patients visited the emergency room during the selected month?
-	What was the average patient wait time?
-	What is the patient satisfaction score?
-	How many patients were admitted vs. not admitted?
-	What is the patient distribution by age group?
-	How is the gender ratio among patients?
-	Which departments receive the highest number of referrals?
-	What percentage of patients were attended on time vs. delayed?

Dashboard Interaction <a href="https://github.com/vipin-s27/Hospital-ER-Data-Analysis/blob/main/Dashboard_Preview.png">View Dashboard</a>

## Process
- Imported raw hospital emergency data into Excel.
- Verified and cleaned data by removing duplicates and fixing missing values.
- Ensured consistency in data types, formats, and values.
- Created pivot tables based on key performance questions.
- Built charts from pivot tables to visualize important metrics.
- Combined all visuals into a single interactive dashboard.
- Applied slicers and filters to make the dashboard dynamic and user-friendly.

## Insights
(Based on data for April 2024 — values may vary for other months)
-	A total of 469 patients visited the emergency room.
-	The average wait time was approximately 35 minutes.
-	Patient satisfaction score averaged 4.63/5, indicating high satisfaction.
-	46% of patients were admitted, while 54% were not admitted.
-	The 20–29 age group had the highest number of visits.
-	General Practice received the most department referrals.
-	57% of patients were attended on time, improving overall response efficiency.
-	The gender ratio was balanced — 51% female and 49% male.

## Final Conclusion:
The Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard provides a clear overview of hospital performance, focusing on patient flow, wait times, and satisfaction. The analysis shows a balanced gender ratio and highlights areas such as on-time patient attendance and departmental efficiency. By using this dashboard, hospital management can easily track KPIs, identify bottlenecks, and make data-driven decisions to improve overall patient experience and operational performance.
